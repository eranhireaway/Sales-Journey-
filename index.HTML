Two Methods to Engage Clients

Phase 01 & 02 — How prospects find LeadStrong

These two phases run independently. A prospect may arrive through outreach, or directly through the website. Both paths lead to the same strategy call.

Method 01 — Proactive
Pre-Show / Digital Outreach
Outreach sequence via Alta.ai
3-touch email + LinkedIn campaign via Instantly and Alta.ai, targeting verified ICP contacts from Apollo. Every message focuses on generating engagements and responses — never revenue promises. This is what makes the first impression different from every other vendor in the prospect’s inbox.
Alta.ai Instantly Apollo lists 3 touches · 12 days monday.com → New Lead (pending)
Method 02 — Inbound
Website CTA
Pricing page + 3-step CTA form
Four service tiers with “starting from” pricing. Prospect selects a tier — the selection is logged immediately. Two qualifying questions with Back/Next navigation, then email capture. The tier they clicked on the pricing page is tracked and compared against the scored best-fit tier in Phase 3.
4 tiers · Base Camp to Summit Change plan at any step Self-selected tier logged monday.com → LS: Leads (pending)
Both methods lead here
Phase 03
Emailed After Booking
Pre-sales qualification + silent scoring
11 questions emailed after the strategy call is booked. The updated form covers business description, gross annual sales, growth goal, lead channels, monthly conversations, client value, pipeline type (B2B/B2C/niche/on-demand), capacity to handle growth, tech comfort (1–5 scale), tech champion, and full-service interest. The prospect sees a clean form — nothing signals that scoring is happening. On submit, all 11 answers run through the tier scoring engine silently. The rep receives a fully ranked proposal brief in monday.com with best-fit tier, confidence levels, self-selected vs scored match flag, HireAway capacity flag, and full-service interest flag. The strategy call becomes a closing call, not a discovery call.
11 questions · ~7 minutes Silent tier scoring engine Annual sales + growth goal signals Pipeline type · Tech comfort · B2B/B2C Self-select vs scored: Match / Mismatch HireAway flag · Full service flag monday.com → LS: Pre-Sales Briefs (pending)
★
Rep advantage: Opens the monday.com brief 10 minutes before the call. Knows the ranked tier recommendation, confidence level, annual sales, growth ambition, pipeline type, tech comfort score, capacity flag, HireAway flag, full-service interest flag, and any mismatch between what the prospect self-selected and what the scoring engine recommends. The call starts with a personalised proposal — not a question.
Phase 04
Strategy Call
30-minute closing call
The rep arrives with a fully scored brief and a personalised proposal already drafted by the AI Proposal Agent (Tab 3 of this tool). The call is structured as a presentation and close — not a discovery session. The rep leads with the best-fit tier recommendation, presents the 90-day roadmap, and addresses the prospect’s specific goal using their own words. HireAway Phase 2 is introduced only if the capacity flag was triggered.
30 minutes Proposal pre-built by AI agent Lead with ranked tier recommendation HireAway Phase 2 — only if flagged monday.com → Status: Active or Unqualified
Phase 05
Post Sign-up · All Tiers
ICP brief — onboarding deliverable
Sent automatically after signing. This is a service deliverable, not a gate. The client completes a thorough ICP brief — industry, titles, geography, problem statement, value proposition — framed as “let’s build your target list together.” Feeds directly into Apollo list-building, campaign copy, and outreach sequence targeting. Triggered automatically by monday.com deal creation.
All tiers Auto-triggered on deal creation Feeds Apollo list build monday.com → Deal board (pending)
Phase 06
Post Sign-up · Ascent + Summit
Outreach audit
Sent to The Ascent and The Summit clients before campaign build. Surfaces past outreach attempts, response rates, tone guidelines, and what a “good response” looks like to them. Prevents repeating failed tactics and surfaces winning messaging angles that can be built into the sequence from day one. Informs copy, subject lines, and CTA strategy.
The Ascent & The Summit only Past outreach & response rates Informs sequence copy Tone & messaging guardrails
Phase 07
Day 30 / Day 90
Results check-in + HireAway upsell
Day 30: delivery quality and client satisfaction check — auto-reminder in monday.com. Day 90: if pipeline results are strong and the capacity flag was triggered at Phase 3, introduce HireAway as a natural Phase 2 capacity solution. HireAway is never mentioned before results are proven. The monday.com automation creates a HireAway Pipeline item and notifies the rep at Day 90.
Day 30 delivery check Day 90 upsell window HireAway — only with proven results monday.com → HireAway Pipeline (pending)

The rep introduces HireAway not as an upsell but as the natural answer to the question every successful client asks — “how do we handle all this volume?” This is where LeadStrong becomes a long-term growth partner, not just a campaign vendor.

Phase 03 — Emailed after booking

Pre-Sales Qualification Form

11 questions. 7 minutes. Your answers are scored silently — the rep receives a ranked proposal brief with confidence levels automatically before the call.

How this works: Emailed after booking. Scoring runs silently on submit — the prospect sees only a clean thank-you. The rep’s brief (right panel) is internal only.
